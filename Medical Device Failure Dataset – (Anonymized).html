About Dataset

Medical Device Failure Dataset – Anonymized Clinical Data (Structured + NLP)

🧠 Context

This dataset was curated based on patterns observed from multiple anonymized hospital equipment maintenance records, clinical engineering reports, and simulated incident logs. The schema and attributes follow typical structures seen in device failure prediction research and biomedical informatics.

🗃️ Description

The dataset contains structured fields like operational hours, manufacturer details, downtime duration, and failure event frequency, as well as unstructured maintenance notes processed via NLP techniques like TF-IDF and LDA topic modeling.

⚠️ Note: To maintain data privacy and adhere to regulatory policies, sensitive details have been masked or replaced with anonymized values.

📊 Columns

  • Manufacturer, Device_Type, Operational_Hours, Downtime, Maintenance_Cost, Failure_Event_Count, Service_Contract, Hospital_Category, and more.

🔍 Inspiration

Inspired by healthcare datasets made available through:

  • FDA’s MAUDE Database
  • ECRI's equipment safety alerts
  • Biomedical Engineering Research Papers (via PubMed/IEEE)

📌 Intended Use

  • Binary classification of failure risk
  • Feature selection and NLP modeling
  • Medical device failure prediction pipelines

📢 Disclaimer

Due to privacy constraints and ethical considerations, this dataset uses anonymized values based on generalized real-world patterns. It should not be interpreted as raw clinical data but can be effectively used for modeling and research purposes.

This dataset was compiled during an internal research initiative focused on preventive maintenance modeling for ICU equipment. The project involved discussions with biomedical engineers and clinical technicians.
